Software Engineer

I have been intrigued by computers since high school, when I assembled first PC using old computers that were not working properly. My tech career kicked off in IT support, a job I got through pure passion and a knack for technology. That role became playground for learning, tweaking systems, and immersing myself in every aspect of technology I needed to master. It sparked my love for programming and building tools that actually help people.

What drives me is the excitement of solving problems and the joy of seeing someone genuinely satisfied with the work I have done. For me, it is all about improving things, whether that means fixing a bug or developing something entirely new.

In 2023, I took my skills to the next level by attending a programming bootcamp, where I immersed myself in the latest technologies. Coding has turned into something I genuinely enjoy and find rewarding. My goal is to participate in creating technology that makes a difference and keeps users happy.
